Harry Rendell-Bhatti

I am a PhD Student at the Royal Observatory, University of Edinburgh. I work under the supervision of Andy Lawrence. We are studying quasar variability through large scale photometric surveys.

STARLITE - SevenTy yeAR LIghT curvEs

STARLITE is a photometric database of observations of 500,000 quasars and 400,000 stars, spanning 70 years. I have compiled this database throughout my PhD and have been using it to investigate quasar variability over long timescales.
